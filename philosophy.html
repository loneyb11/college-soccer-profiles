PHILOSOPHY

The SA United philosophy is the core of the club. It is who we are and why we do the things we do. We strive to the best grassroots club in the San Antonio area, and we care deeply about developing young players on and off the field. Lessons learned from the time spent with the club, will serve our players when they step out into the world. Core values such as leadership, development, positivity, passion, energy, drive, humility and the desire to be a good person are at the front of what we stand for.


Our coaching philosophy is player centered, meaning this is about the players, not the parents and not the coach. Everything we do, every decision made, is with the best interest of the players at heart.


Our playing style involves possession and build up. Not kick the ball and hope for the best. We place players in scenarios that put them under pressure, whilst learning about spacing and positional play. Positional play will teach the players how to break lines, how to move off the ball, how to be more aware of the spaces around them, work on tactical concepts such as wide play, combinations and 3rd player runs, how to build through the 3rds in a controlled manner, and how to deal with the ever-changing game situations, solve problems and play under pressure. We aim to develop intelligent, thinking soccer players, and therefore training sessions must be structured to mirror the demands of the match.

In soccer, we have a similar process, where the younger players are first taught some basic skills such as how to pass, dribble and shoot, before moving onto the next stage where they begin to learn and spacing, positional play and more tactical elements.
