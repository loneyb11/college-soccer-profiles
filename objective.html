OBJECTIVE

The objective of the SA United club curriculum is to provide insight into the way that SA United teaches the game of soccer through various principles. It provides coaches with information on our playing style, philosophy, game model and more, which in turn standardizes our training sessions and aligns the club to a specific process of developing of players.


Instead of teams being ‘individualized’ and coaches working on random topics within the club, our club curriculum will aim to bring together age groups across the board and allow our coaches to educate our players using similar training themes and topics which we believe at SA United are the right way to teach the game.


Coaches can use the curriculum as a guide as they provide a quality and enjoyable training environment for every player in our club, from our Rising Stars program all the way up to our U19 senior teams.
